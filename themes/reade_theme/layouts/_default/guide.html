Getting Started

Before you begin, I highly recommend becoming familiar with The Five Fundamental Principles of NeverFap.

These principles will form the basis of everything you will learn, and each article will focus on a particular principle.

Learn more about the five principles.

Articles are organised into two types.

There are contextual articles, which are designed to provide you with perspective and knowlege in order for you to gain a better understand of yourself.

In addition there are practice articles, which focus solely on verifiable steps you can take on a daily basis to help you progress towards learning the skills to end porn addiction.

You know, you can't become an amazing pianist or the world's best guitarist by staring at sheet music all day, and the same goes for overcoming porn addiction.

Without training your mind and learning new skills through targeted exercises, you can only hope to succumb yourself to the excesses of trial and error.
